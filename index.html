About

Myself

Hi! This is Robin.


I am Robin Mathew Abraham, a dedicated and results-driven Systems Engineer with over 3 years of experience in developing and maintaining critical applications. My passion lies in leveraging my expertise in Java, SQL database management, Quality Assurance, and customer relationship building to contribute to a progressive organization. I am committed to continuous learning and growth, aiming to achieve excellence in my career. With a strong track record of delivering solutions and optimizing performance, I possess excellent troubleshooting skills and the ability to identify root causes and provide timely resolutions. I have successfully collaborated with teams and actively contributed to process automation and bug resolution. My educational background includes a Master of Computer Applications (MCA) from the College of Engineering, Trivandrum, and a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSc. CS) from the College of Applied Sciences, Adoor. I am fluent in English, Malayalam (native speaker), and have a good command of Tamil.
